🧠 Critical Thinking Prompts

As you watch the video, think about the following questions. We will discuss these in our upcoming debate:

  • 1
    Stakeholder Perspectives: Who are the primary beneficiaries of a statutory minimum wage? Who bears the cost?
  • 2
    Unintended Consequences: According to economic theories, how might employers react if the minimum wage is set significantly higher than the market equilibrium?
  • 3
    Real-world Application: Do you think the current minimum wage in Hong Kong is "effective" (binding) in today's labor market? Why or why not?

Real-world Connection:
