🧠 Critical Thinking Prompts

As you watch the video, think about the following questions based on the concepts discussed. We will debate these in class:

  • 1
    Weighing Pros and Cons: The video highlights that price floors provide financial security for producers, but can also lead to wasteful surpluses and inefficiency. In your opinion, do the protections for workers/producers justify the higher prices and potential waste for consumers?
  • 2
    Unintended Market Outcomes: When a price floor is set too high (like an excessive minimum wage), businesses might not be able to afford as many workers. How does this consequence specifically impact the people the policy was originally trying to help?
  • 3
    Evaluating Alternatives: The video suggests direct government subsidies and tax incentives as alternatives to a price floor. How do these alternatives achieve the goal of supporting producers and workers without artificially throwing the market equilibrium out of balance?
